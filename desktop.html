Investment Criteria

Target Profile

We focus on established companies with proven business models, strong market positions, and exceptional management teams positioned for accelerated growth.

Our investment criteria are designed to identify companies with sustainable competitive advantages and clear paths to value creation through strategic and operational improvements.

Company Stage

Growth-stage companies with proven business models, established customer bases, and clear scalability. Typically Series B+ for venture investments or established companies for buyouts.

Financial Profile

Revenue range of $10M-$250M with strong unit economics, positive cash flow or clear path to profitability, and demonstrated ability to scale operations efficiently.

Investment Size

Initial investments between $5M-$50M with ability to provide follow-on capital for continued growth. Flexible structure including equity, debt, and hybrid instruments.
